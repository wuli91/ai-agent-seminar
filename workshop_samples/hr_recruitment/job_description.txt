# Job Description: Senior Fullstack Developer (Backend Focus)

## Overview
Thinkware is looking for a Senior Fullstack Developer to join our Core Platform team. You will be responsible for designing scalable backend services and ensuring seamless integration with our frontend applications.

## Key Requirements
- **Technical Skills:**
    - Expert proficiency in **Java** and **Spring Boot**.
    - Strong experience with **SQL** databases (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL).
    - Proficiency in modern frontend frameworks, specifically **React** or **Vue.js**.
    - Experience with **Cloud environments** (AWS/Azure/GCP).
    - Understanding of **Microservices** architecture and **Docker/Kubernetes**.
- **Soft Skills:**
    - Ability to lead small teams and mentor junior developers.
    - Excellent problem-solving skills and architectural thinking.
    - Strong communication skills for cross-functional collaboration.

## Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with **TypeScript** and **Node.js**.
- Familiarity with **Agile/Scrum** methodologies.
- Experience with **DevOps** practices (CI/CD pipelines).

## Work Conditions
- **Location:** Seoul (Hybrid)
- **Notice Period:** Ideally 1 month or less.
