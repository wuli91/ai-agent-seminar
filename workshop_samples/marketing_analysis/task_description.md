# Workshop Task: Amazon Dashcam Competitive Positioning Analysis

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze how different Dashcam products are positioned on Amazon by comparing their **Price** and **Key Features** across multiple dimensions. This exercise simulates a real-world marketing research task using Claude.

## 🛠️ Task Scenario
You are a Marketing Analyst at a tech company. You need to understand where our (hypothetical) new dashcam fits in the current market.

## 📝 Requirements

### 1. Research Dimensions (The "Axes")
Analyze the products based on the following criteria:
*   **Price Point:** (Budget vs. Mid-range vs. Premium)
*   **Key Features:** 
    *   Resolution (e.g., 1080p, 2K, 4K)
    *   Night Vision capability
    *   Parking Mode (G-sensor/Hardwire required)
    *   Special Features (e.g., Cloud storage, GPS, Dual-channel/Rear camera)
*   **Brand Perception:** (Value-focused vs. Feature-rich/High-end)

### 2. Expected Deliverables (Report Structure)
Your final report should include:
1.  **Competitor Matrix:** A table comparing at least 5 top-selling Dashcams across the dimensions above.
2.  **Perceptual Map (Text-based):** A description of how products cluster (e.g., "High Price / High Feature" vs. "Low Price / Basic Features").
3.  **Market Gap Analysis:** Identify an underserved segment (e.g., "There is a gap for a 4K camera with easy Cloud storage at a mid-range price point").
4.  **Strategic Recommendation:** Suggest one specific feature or pricing strategy for our new product to win market share.

### 3. Workshop Workflow (How to use Claude)
*   **Step 1 (Data Collection):** Use Claude to "search" or "simulate" a search for top-rated Dashcams on Amazon.
*   **Step 2 (Data Extraction):** Extract specific technical specs and price points from the search results.
*   **Step 3 (Analysis):** Ask Claude to "Synthesize this data into a competitive positioning matrix."
*   **Step 4 (Reporting):** Use a prompt like: *"Based on the matrix, write a professional marketing report identifying market gaps and strategic recommendations for a new dashcam entry."*
